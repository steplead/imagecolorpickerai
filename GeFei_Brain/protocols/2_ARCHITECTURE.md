# Protocol 2: Site Architecture (Classified Listing)

> **Core Rule:** "Structure > Content Quantity."

## 1. The "Classified Listing" Strategy (分门别类)
Do not build a "Blog". Build a "Library".
*   **Concept:** Google rewards pages that **Aggregate** resources.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   **Category Page:** `/ai-tools/` (Lists 50 tools).
    *   **Tag Page:** `/ai-tools/free/` (Lists 10 free tools).
    *   **Detail Page:** `/ai-tools/chatgpt-review` (The specific content).

## 2. The Power of Tags
Tags are your #1 weapon for "Programmatic SEO".
*   If you have 100 items, create 20 tags (Color, Size, Price, OS).
*   This generates **20 x 5 = 100** unique Landing Pages automatically.
*   *Library Quote:* "Listing pages (Classified) have higher weight than single articles."

## 3. Internal Linking Hierarchy
*   **Home** -> **Category** -> **Detail**
*   **Detail** -> **Related Tag** -> **Home**
*   *Never* leave a "Orphan Page" (Page with 0 links).

## 4. URL Structure
*   ✅ `domain.com/category/keyword` (Clean, shows hierarchy)
*   ✅ `domain.com/keyword` (Short, powerful for top terms)
*   ❌ `domain.com/2024/12/10/keyword` (Dates kill evergreen value)
